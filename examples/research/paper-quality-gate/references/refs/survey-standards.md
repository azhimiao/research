# 综述与交付标准

完整方法论见 `refs/authority-standards.md`、`refs/search-protocol.md`、`refs/synthesis-methods.md`、`refs/citation-verification.md`、`refs/quality-rubric.md`。

## Skill 分工

| Skill | 职责 |
|-------|------|
| **research-survey** | 入口编排：选 mode → 三阶段 → 交付 |
| **literature-survey** | 检索、筛选、验证、文献库 |
| **review-synthesis** | 叙事综述、证据矩阵、gap |
| **paper-quality-gate** | 可信度审计、quality-report |

## survey-report.md 大纲

1. Executive Summary
2. Research Question & Framework (PICO/SPIDER/PEO)
3. Methods — Search & Screening（链到 search-protocol / screening-log）
4. Landscape — thematic clusters
5. Consensus & Open Debates
6. Gaps & Future Work
7. Hypothesis Candidates（mode 含 hypothesis）
8. Roadmap（mode end_to_end）
9. Quality Gate Summary（来自 quality-report.json）
10. Limitations of This Survey
11. Source Index（仅 verified/partial）

## 引用纪律

- 零编造；验证协议见 citation-verification.md
- UNVERIFIED 不得支撑 factual claim
- strict gate：author sign-off 必填
